Ring of Three Wishes

  • Rarity: Legendary
  • Type: Ring
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: 5.2 SRD (D&D 2024)

While wearing this ring, you can expend 1 of its 3 charges to cast Wish from it. The ring becomes nonmagical when you use the last charge.
