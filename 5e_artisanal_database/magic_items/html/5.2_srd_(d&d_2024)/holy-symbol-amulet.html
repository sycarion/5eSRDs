Holy Symbol, Amulet)

  • Rarity: Varies
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: 5.2 SRD (D&D 2024)

A Holy Symbol Amulet is bejeweled or painted to channel divine magic. A Cleric or Paladin can use it as a Spellcasting Focus. It can be worn around the neck or held in hand.
