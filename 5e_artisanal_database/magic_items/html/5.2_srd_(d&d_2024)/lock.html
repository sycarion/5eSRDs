Lock

  • Rarity: Varies
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: 5.2 SRD (D&D 2024)

A Lock comes with a key. Without the key, a creature can use Thieves' Tools to pick this Lock with a successful DC 15 Dexterity (Sleight of Hand) check.
