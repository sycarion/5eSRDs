Circlet of Blasting

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: 5.2 SRD (D&D 2024)

While wearing this circlet, you can cast Scorching Ray with it (+5 to hit). The circlet can't cast this spell again until the next dawn.
