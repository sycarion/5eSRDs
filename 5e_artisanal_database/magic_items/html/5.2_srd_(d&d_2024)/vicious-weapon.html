Vicious Weapon

  • Rarity: Rare
  • Type: Weapon
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: 5.2 SRD (D&D 2024)

This magic weapon deals an extra 2d6 damage to any creature it hits. This extra damage is of the same type as the weapon's normal damage.
