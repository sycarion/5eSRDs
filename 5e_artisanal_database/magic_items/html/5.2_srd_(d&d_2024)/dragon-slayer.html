Dragon Slayer

  • Rarity: Rare
  • Type: Weapon
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: 5.2 SRD (D&D 2024)

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The weapon deals an extra 3d6 damage of the weapon's type if the target is a Dragon.
