Wand of Magic Detection

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wand
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: 5.2 SRD (D&D 2024)

This wand has 3 charges. While holding it, you can expend 1 charge to cast Detect Magic from it. The wand regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn.
