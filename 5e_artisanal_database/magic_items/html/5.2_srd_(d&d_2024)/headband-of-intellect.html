Headband of Intellect

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: 5.2 SRD (D&D 2024)

Your Intelligence is 19 while you wear this headband. It has no effect on you if your Intelligence is 19 or higher without it.
