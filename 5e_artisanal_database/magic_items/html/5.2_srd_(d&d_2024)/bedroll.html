Bedroll

  • Rarity: Varies
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: 5.2 SRD (D&D 2024)

A Bedroll sleeps one Small or Medium creature. While in a Bedroll, you automatically succeed on saving throws against extreme cold (see "Gameplay Toolbox").
