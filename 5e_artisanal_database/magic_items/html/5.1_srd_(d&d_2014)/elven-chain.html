Elven Chain

  • Rarity: Rare
  • Type: Armor
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: 5.1 SRD (D&D 2014)

You gain a +1 bonus to AC while you wear this armor. You are considered proficient with this armor even if you lack proficiency with medium armor.
