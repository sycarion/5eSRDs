Ring of Telekinesis

  • Rarity: Very Rare
  • Type: Ring
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: 5.1 SRD (D&D 2014)

While wearing this ring, you can cast the telekinesis spell at will, but you can target only objects that aren't being worn or carried.
