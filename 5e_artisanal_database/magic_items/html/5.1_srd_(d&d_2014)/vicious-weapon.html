Vicious Weapon

  • Rarity: Rare
  • Type: Weapon
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: 5.1 SRD (D&D 2014)

When you roll a 20 on your attack roll with this magic weapon, your critical hit deals an extra 2d6 damage of the weapon's type.
