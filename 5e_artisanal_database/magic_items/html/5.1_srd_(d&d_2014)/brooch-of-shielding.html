Brooch of Shielding

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: 5.1 SRD (D&D 2014)

While wearing this brooch, you have resistance to force damage, and you have immunity to damage from the magic missile spell.
