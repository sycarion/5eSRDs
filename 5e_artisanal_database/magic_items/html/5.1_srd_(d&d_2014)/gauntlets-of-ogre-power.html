Gauntlets of Ogre Power

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: 5.1 SRD (D&D 2014)

Your Strength score is 19 while you wear these gauntlets. They have no effect on you if your Strength is already 19 or higher.
