Ivy Crown of Prophecy

  • Rarity: Rare
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Vault of Magic

While wearing this ivy, filigreed crown, you can use an action to cast the divination spell from it. The crown can't be used this way again until the next dawn.
