Corpsehunter's Medallion

  • Rarity: Rare
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Vault of Magic

This amulet is made from the skulls of grave rats or from scrimshawed bones of the ignoble dead. While wearing it, you have resistance to necrotic damage.
