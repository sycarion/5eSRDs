Dirk of Daring

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Weapon
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: Vault of Magic

You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. While holding the dagger, you have advantage on saving throws against being frightened.
