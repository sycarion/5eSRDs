Medal of Valor

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Vault of Magic

You are immune to the frightened condition while you wear this medal. If you are already frightened, the effect ends immediately when you put on the medal.
