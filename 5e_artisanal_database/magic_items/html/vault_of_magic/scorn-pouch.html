Scorn Pouch

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: Vault of Magic

The heart of a lover scorned turns black and potent. Similarly, this small leather pouch darkens from brown to black when a creature hostile to you moves within 10 feet of you.
