Glazed Blade

  • Rarity: Common
  • Type: Weapon
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Vault of Magic

A pleasant scent emerges from this weapon. While it is on your person, you have advantage on Charisma (Persuasion) checks made to interact with humanoids and fey.
