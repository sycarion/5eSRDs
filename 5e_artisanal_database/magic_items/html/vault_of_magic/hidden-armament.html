Hidden Armament

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Weapon
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: Vault of Magic

While holding this magic weapon, you can use an action to transform it into a tattoo on the palm of your hand. You can use a bonus action to transform the tattoo back into a weapon.
