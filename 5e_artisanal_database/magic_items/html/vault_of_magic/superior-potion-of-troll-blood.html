Superior Potion of Troll Blood

  • Rarity: Very Rare
  • Type: Potion
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Vault of Magic

When you drink this potion, you regain 5 hit points at the start of each of your turns. After it has restored 50 hit points, the potion's effects end.
