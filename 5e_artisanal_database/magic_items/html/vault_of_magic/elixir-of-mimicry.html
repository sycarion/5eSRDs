Elixir of Mimicry

  • Rarity: Common
  • Type: Potion
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Vault of Magic

When you drink this sweet, oily, black liquid, you can imitate the voice of a single creature that you have heard speak within the past 24 hours. The effects last for 3 minutes.
