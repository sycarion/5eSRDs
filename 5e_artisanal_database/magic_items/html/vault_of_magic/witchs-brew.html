Witch's Brew

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Potion
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Vault of Magic

For 1 minute after drinking this potion, your spell attacks deal an extra 1d4 necrotic damage on a hit. This revolting green potion's opaque liquid bubbles and steams as if boiling.
