Greater Potion of Troll Blood

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Potion
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Vault of Magic

When drink this potion, you regain 3 hit points at the start of each of your turns. After it has restored 30 hit points, the potion's effects end.
