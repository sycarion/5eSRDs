Chieftain's Axe

  • Rarity: Very Rare
  • Type: Weapon
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: Vault of Magic

Furs conceal the worn runes lining the haft of this oversized, silver-headed battleaxe. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this silvered, magic weapon.
