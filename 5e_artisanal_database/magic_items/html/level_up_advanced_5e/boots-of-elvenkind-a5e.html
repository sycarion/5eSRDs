Boots of Elvenkind

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

These boots cause your steps to make no sound, no matter the material stepped upon. While wearing these boots, you gain advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks to move silently.
