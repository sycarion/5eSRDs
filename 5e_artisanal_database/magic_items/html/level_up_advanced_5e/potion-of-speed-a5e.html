Potion of Speed

  • Rarity: Very Rare
  • Type: Potion
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

An opaque gray liquid that occasionally flashes with sparks of electricity. After drinking this potion, for the next minute you gain the benefits of a haste spell (without the need for concentration ).
