Glove of Swift Return

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

While you are attuned to and wearing this crimson glove, any weapon you throw returns to your gloved hand immediately after it hits or misses the target.
