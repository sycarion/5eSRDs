Hunter's Quiver

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

You can pull an endless number of nonmagical arrows from this quiver. An arrow disappears when it is fired or if it leaves your possession for longer than 1 minute. While you carry the quiver, if no hostile creatures are within 30 feet of you, you can use a bonus action to aim, gaining advantage on ranged weapon attacks until the end of your turn.
