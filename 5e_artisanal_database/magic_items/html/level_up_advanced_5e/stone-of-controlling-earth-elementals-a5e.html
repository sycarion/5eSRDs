Stone of Controlling Earth Elementals

  • Rarity: Rare
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

You can use an action to touch this 5 pound stone to the ground and speak a command word, summoning an earth elemental (as the conjure elemental spell). Once used, the stone cannot be used again until the next dawn.
