Headband of Intellect

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

Wearing this headband increases your Intelligence score to 19. It has no effect if your Intelligence is equal to greater than 19.
