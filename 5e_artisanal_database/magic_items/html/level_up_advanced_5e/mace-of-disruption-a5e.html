Mace of Disruption

  • Rarity: Rare
  • Type: Weapon
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

Fiends and undead take an extra 2d6 radiant damage when hit with this weapon. When this weapon reduces a fiend or undead to 25 or fewer hit points, the creature makes a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or it is destroyed. On a successful save the creature becomes frightened of you until the end of your next turn.

While it is held, this weapon shines bright light in a 20-foot radius and dim light an additional 20 feet.
