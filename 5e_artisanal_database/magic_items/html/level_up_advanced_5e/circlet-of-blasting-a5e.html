Circlet of Blasting

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

Once per dawn, while wearing this circlet you can use an action to cast scorching ray . The attack bonus for the spell when cast this way is +5.
