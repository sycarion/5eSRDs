Liquid Luck

  • Rarity: Legendary
  • Type: Potion
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

This ornate vial contains a liquid that shimmers with opalescent hues. After drinking this potion, for the next 24 hours you have advantage whenever you roll a d20.
