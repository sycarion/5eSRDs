Red Cloak of Riding

  • Rarity: Very Rare
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

While wearing this scarlet cloak, you gain a +2 bonus to AC but have disadvantage on Insight and Perception checks. In addition, when you are reduced to 0 hit points and wearing this cloak, you drop to 1 hit point instead. You can’t use this feature again until you finish a long rest .
