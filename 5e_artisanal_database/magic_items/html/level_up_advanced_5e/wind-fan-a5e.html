Wind Fan

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

You can use an action to wave this fan and cast gust of wind (save DC 13). Each time the fan is used before the next dawn there is a cumulative 20% chance that it breaks, causing the spell to fail and for it to shred into nonmagical pieces.
