Philter of Love

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Potion
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

After drinking this philter, the next time you see a creature in the next 10 minutes, you become charmed by that creature for 1 hour. If you would normally be attracted to the creature, while charmed you believe it to be your true love.
