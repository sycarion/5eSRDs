Ring of Spell Turning

  • Rarity: Legendary
  • Type: Ring
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

While wearing this ring, you have advantage on saving throws against spells that target only you (not an area of effect spell). Additionally, on a saving throw with a natural 20 if the spell is 7th-level or lower, the spell instead targets the caster, using their original slot level, spell save DC, spell attack modifier, and spellcasting ability.
