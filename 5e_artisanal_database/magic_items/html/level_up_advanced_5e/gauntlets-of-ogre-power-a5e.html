Gauntlets of Ogre Power

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

Wearing these gauntlets increases your Strength score to 19. They have no effect if your Strength is equal to or greater than 19.
