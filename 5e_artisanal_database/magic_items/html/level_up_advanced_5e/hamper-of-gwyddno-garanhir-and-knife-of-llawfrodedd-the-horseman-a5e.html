Hamper of Gwyddno Garanhir & Knife of Llawfrodedd the Horseman

  • Rarity: Rare
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

You can use an action to cast create food and water by putting 1d4 Supply into this large wooden basket or using this cutting knife to prepare a 1d4 Supply. You cannot use food created in this way to activate either of these magic items.
