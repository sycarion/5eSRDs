Horseshoes of Speed

  • Rarity: Rare
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

These iron horseshoes come in a set of four. Once all four are affixed to the hooves of a horse or similar creature, its Speed increases by 30 feet.
