Staff of Squalor

  • Rarity: Rare
  • Type: Staff
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

Strands of white mycelium cover the head of this gnarled wooden staff. When tapped on the ground, the staff sheds a thin coating of dirt. While attuned to the staff, you suffer no harmful effects from diseases but can still carry diseases and spread them to others. When you hit a creature with this staff, you can force the target to make a DC 12 Constitution saving throw . On a failure, it contracts one disease of your choice that you’re currently carrying.
