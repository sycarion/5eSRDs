Potion of Heroism

  • Rarity: Rare
  • Type: Potion
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

After drinking this glowing white potion, for the next hour you gain 10 temporary hit points and the benefits of the bless spell (but without the need for concentration ).
