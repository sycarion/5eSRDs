Ring of Invisibility

  • Rarity: Legendary
  • Type: Ring
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

While wearing this ring, you can use an action to turn invisible along with anything you are carrying or wearing. You remain invisible until the ring is removed, you attack or cast a spell, or you use a bonus action to become visible.
