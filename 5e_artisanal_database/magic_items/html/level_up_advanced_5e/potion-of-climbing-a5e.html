Potion of Climbing

  • Rarity: Common
  • Type: Potion
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

After drinking this potion, for the next hour you gain a climbing speed equal to your Speed. While the effect lasts, you have advantage on checks made to climb.
