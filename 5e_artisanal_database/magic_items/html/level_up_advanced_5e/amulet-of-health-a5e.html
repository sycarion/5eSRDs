Amulet of Health

  • Rarity: Rare
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

Wearing this amulet increases your Constitution score to 19. It has no effect if your Constitution is equal to or greater than 19.
