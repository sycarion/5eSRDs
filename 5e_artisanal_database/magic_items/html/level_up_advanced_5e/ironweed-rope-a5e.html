Ironweed Rope

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

When this tough, fibrous plant is carefully woven into rope it has AC 17, 10 hit points, and resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from nonmagical attacks.
