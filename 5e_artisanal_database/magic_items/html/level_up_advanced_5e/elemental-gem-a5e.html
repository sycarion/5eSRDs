Elemental Gem

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

You can use an action to break this gem, releasing a burst of elemental energy that summons an elemental to you as if you had cast the conjure elemental spell. The type of gem determines the elemental summoned.

The gem loses its enchantment when broken.

Table: Elemental Gem

Corundum Fire elemental
Diamond Earth elemental
Emerald Water elemental
Sapphire Air elemental
