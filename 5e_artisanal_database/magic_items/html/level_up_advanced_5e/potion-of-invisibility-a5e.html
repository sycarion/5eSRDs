Potion of Invisibility

  • Rarity: Very Rare
  • Type: Potion
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

The silver liquid in this vial is visible only when shaken or disturbed. After drinking this potion, for the next hour you and anything you’re wearing or carrying becomes invisible . The effect ends early if you attack or cast a spell.
