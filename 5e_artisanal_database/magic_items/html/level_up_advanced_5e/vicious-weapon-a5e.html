Vicious Weapon

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Weapon
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

When you roll a natural 20 on an attack roll using this weapon it deals an extra 2d6 damage of the weapon’s type (this extra damage does not double).
