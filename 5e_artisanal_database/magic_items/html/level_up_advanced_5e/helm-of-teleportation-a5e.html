Helm of Teleportation

  • Rarity: Very Rare
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

While wearing this helm you can use an action to cast teleport . The helm has 3 charges and regains 1d3 charges each dawn.
