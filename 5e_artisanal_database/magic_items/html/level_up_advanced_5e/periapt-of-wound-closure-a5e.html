Periapt of Wound Closure

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

Engraved with symbols of healing, this warm silver pendant prevents you from dying.

  • While wearing this pendant, you automatically stabilize when dying. You may still roll your Death saving throw to try and roll a 20, but may only roll once on each of your turns and no more than three times.
  • Whenever you roll a Hit Die to regain hit points, you regain twice as many as normal.
