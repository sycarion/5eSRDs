Necklace of Adaptation

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

While wearing this finely-woven necklace, you can breathe normally in any environment and you have advantage on saving throws made against harmful gases, vapors, and other inhaled effects.
