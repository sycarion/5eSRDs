Restorative Ointment

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

This green glass jar holds 1d4+1 doses of a fresh-smelling mixture. You can use an action to swallow or apply one dose, restoring 2d8+2 hit points and curing both poison and disease .
