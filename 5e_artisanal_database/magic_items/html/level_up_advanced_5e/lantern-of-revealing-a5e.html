Lantern of Revealing

  • Rarity: Rare
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

While lit, this hooded lantern casts bright light in a 30-foot radius, revealing invisible creatures and objects in the area. It sheds dim light an additional 30 feet, or if you use an action to lower the hood only in a 5-foot radius. The lantern stays lit for 6 hours on 1 pint of oil.
