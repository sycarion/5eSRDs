Gong of Alarm

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up Advanced 5e

As an action, you can cast the __alarm_ spell through this brass gong. When cast this way, the spell’s duration becomes 1 month. The gong can’t be used to cast alarm again while the spell is active and for 24 hours thereafter.
