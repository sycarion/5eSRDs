# Cloak of Shadow

- **Level**: 1 illusion
- **Classes**: 
- **Casting Time**: 1 bonus action
- **Range**: Self
- **Components**: V
- **Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute
- **Source**: Deep Magic 5e

You cloak yourself in shadow, giving you advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks against creatures that rely on sight.
