# Guest of Honor

- **Level**: 1 enchantment
- **Classes**: Bard, Wizard
- **Casting Time**: 10 minutes
- **Range**: Touch
- **Components**: V, M (a signet ring worth 25 gp)
- **Duration**: 10 minutes
- **Source**: Deep Magic 5e

You whisper words of encouragement, and a creature that you touch gains confidence along with approval from strangers. For the spellâ€™s duration, the target puts its best foot forward and strangers associate the creature with positive feelings. The target adds 1d4 to all Charisma (Persuasion) checks made to influence the attitudes of others.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the effect lasts for an additional 10 minutes for each slot level above 1st.

***Ritual Focus.*** If you expend your ritual focus, the effect lasts for 24 hours.
