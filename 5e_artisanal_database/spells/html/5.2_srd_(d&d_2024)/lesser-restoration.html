Lesser Restoration

  • Level: 2 Abjuration
  • Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Ranger
  • Casting Time: 1 bonus action
  • Range: Touch
  • Components: V, S
  • Duration: instantaneous
  • Source: 5.2 SRD (D&D 2024)

You touch a creature and end one condition on it: Blinded, Deafened, Paralyzed, or Poisoned.
