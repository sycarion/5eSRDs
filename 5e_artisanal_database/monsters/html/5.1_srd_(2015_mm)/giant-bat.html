Giant Bat

Large Beast unaligned

  • Hit Points: 22 (4d10)
  • Speed: walk 10 ft. fly 60 ft.
  • Challenge Rating: 1/4 (50 XP)
  • Source: 5.1 SRD (2015 MM), page 373
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
