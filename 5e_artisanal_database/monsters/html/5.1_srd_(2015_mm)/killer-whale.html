Killer Whale

Huge Beast unaligned

  • Hit Points: 90 (12d12+12)
  • Speed: swim 60 ft.
  • Challenge Rating: 3 (700 XP)
  • Source: 5.1 SRD (2015 MM), page 383
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
