# Magic Missile

- **Level**: 1 Evocation
- **Classes**: Sorcerer, Wizard
- **Casting Time**: 1 action
- **Range**: 120 feet
- **Components**: V, S
- **Duration**: Instantaneous
- **Source**: Level Up Advanced 5e

A trio of glowing darts of magical force unerringly and simultaneously strike the targets, each dealing 1d4+1 force damage.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** Evoke one additional dart and target up to one additional creature for each slot level above 1st.
